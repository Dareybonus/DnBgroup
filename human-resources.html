what we do

D & B human resource specialists are responsible for recruiting, screening, interviewing and placing workers. We also handle employee relations, payroll, benefits, and training. Our managers plan, direct and coordinate the administrative functions of an organization. We oversee specialists in their duties; consult with executives on strategic planning, and link a company's management with its employees.

Training

Training is crucial for organizational development and success. It is fruitful to both employers and employees of an organization. Training of employees takes place after orientation takes place. Our HR team at D & B, offer several training of new and old employees. This training helps mould the thinking of employees and leads to quality performance of employees. It is continuous and never ending in nature.

Recruitment

At DEALS & BRIDGES, we help organizations with overall process of identifying, sourcing, screening, shortlisting, and interviewing candidates for jobs within an organization. Our Managers, human resource generalists and recruitment specialists have vast experience in carrying out recruitment, but in some cases public-sector employment, commercial recruitment agencies, or specialist search consultancies.

Staff Compensation

Staff compensation/Remuneration leads to employee motivation. Salaries constitutes an important source of income for employees and determine their standard of living. Salaries effect the employees productivity and work performance. Thus the amount and method of remuneration are very important for both management and employees. At DEALS & BRIDGES we ensure employees are rewarded for their selfless services. We practice two methods of staff renumeration. 1. Time rate method and 2. Piece rate method. These two methods are efficiently used to make sure all employees both old and new are duely compensated.
